<h2 id="-">第一部分：開始遊戲</h2><p>Go to the <a target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener" href="https://arcade.makecode.com/">MakeCode Arcade</a> editor and select <strong>New Project</strong> to begin creating a new game.</p>
<p>Once the editor loads, you will see a green <code>||loops:on start||</code> block already placed in the editor Workspace.</p>
<p>We want the game to start with a <em>Title Screen</em> to let the player know what game they are playing. To do that, we go to <code>||game:Game||</code> in the Toolbox and under the <strong>Prompt</strong> category, drag a <code>||game:splash||</code> block into the <code>||loops:on start||</code> block. Give your game a title and put in as the text for the <code>||game:splash||</code> block.</p>
<pre><code class="lang-blocks">game.splash(&quot;Cherry Pickr&quot;)</code></pre>
<h2 id="-">第二部分：設定背景</h2><p>To set the background, click on <code>||scene:Scene||</code> in the Toolbox and drag the <code>||scene:set tilemap to||</code> block into the <code>||loops:on start||</code> block. <h2 id="-">第六部分：撿起櫻桃</h2><p>The last task is to let our player to actually collect cherries. <p>This block will run every time a <code>||sprites:Player||</code> sprite overlaps a <code>||sprites:Food||</code> sprite.</p>
<h3 id="changing-the-score">Changing the Score</h3><p>To change the score when the cherry is touched, go to the <code>||info:Info||</code> tab and drag the <code>||info:change score by||</code> block into the <code>||sprites:on sprite of kind Player overlaps||</code> block. Leave the value at <code>1</code>.</p>
<h3 id="removing-the-item">Removing the Item</h3><p>When this block runs, two new variables are created: <code>||variables:sprite||</code> and <code>||variables:otherSprite||</code>. <p>To do so, go to <code>||sprites:Sprites||</code> in the Toolbox and, under <strong>Lifecycle</strong>, drag the <code>||sprites:destroy||</code> block into the <code>||sprites:on sprite of kind Player overlaps||</code> block. <p>You might notice that the <code>||variables:mySprite||</code> block is removed from the <code>||sprites:destroy||</code> block when you replace it with <code>||variables:otherSprite||</code>. You can delete that block by dragging it to the Toolbox.</p>
